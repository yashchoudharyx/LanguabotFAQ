What is LinguaPal?

LinguaPal is a chatbot that helps users translate phrases and identify languages.

What languages does it support?

English, French, Spanish, Japanese, German, and Hindi.

Can it translate full sentences?

No, it works best with short phrases like greetings or thank you.

How do I use it?

Type a phrase like “Translate hello to Spanish” or “What does bonjour mean?”

Does it store any data?

No, it does not store user information.
